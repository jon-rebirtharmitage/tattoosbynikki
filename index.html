Tattoos and Art

Tattoos and artwork in Iowa City, Iowa.

Nikki is a lover of all kinds of arts, crafts, and creativity. Through her early life you could commonly find her painting, drawing ,sculpting , or crafting jewelry, all things she still enjoys today! She started tattooing in early February 2007, just out of high school, and began a 3 year apprenticeship at Exile Tattoo. Shortly after completing her apprenticeship she left in 2011 and started to work at Vesania Ink, where she's currently located. Follow her on Facebook or instagram to view more work and get regular updates on projects and tattoos.
